Natural Language Input
What was our MRR last 6 months broken down by plan?
↓ Generated SQL
Executed Query
-- TetherDust: MRR by plan, last 6 months SELECT DATE_TRUNC('month', s.created_at) AS month, p.name AS plan, SUM(s.amount_cents) / 100.0 AS mrr FROM subscriptions s JOIN plans p ON p.id = s.plan_id WHERE s.status = 'active' AND s.created_at >= NOW() - INTERVAL '6 months' GROUP BY 1, 2 ORDER BY 1 ASC, mrr DESC;
