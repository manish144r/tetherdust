What TetherDust Does

Everything your data team needs.
Nothing they don't.

Built for engineers and analysts who want power without the enterprise price tag or the vendor lock-in.

Natural Language to SQL

Type questions like you'd ask a colleague. TetherDust converts plain English to production-quality SQL in milliseconds, using your actual schema as context. Supports complex joins, window functions, and aggregations.

6 Data Source Connectors

Connect Postgres, BigQuery, Snowflake, Redshift, MySQL, and DuckDB out of the box. Schema-aware context injection means the AI always understands your actual table structure and column semantics.

Auto-Generated Charts

Results are automatically visualized as line charts, bar charts, scatter plots, or tables depending on the query shape. Export to PNG, SVG, or embed in Notion and Confluence with a single link.

Zero Data Leaves Your Infra

The LLM only ever sees your schema and query results — not your raw data. Self-hosted mode runs entirely on-prem. GDPR, HIPAA, and SOC 2 friendly by architecture, not by policy document.
